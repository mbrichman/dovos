About Project Own
Project Own is a small and growing tech nonprofit based in Baltimore, Maryland, dedicated to transforming access to homeownership and wealth creation. We're building innovative solutions at the intersection of technology and financial inclusion, working to break down barriers that prevent individuals and families from achieving their homeownership goals and building generational wealth.

‍

The Role
We're seeking a Fractional Head of Product to lead and scale our product development. This is a unique opportunity to steer product strategy and development with an early stage, in-the-market product at a growing nonprofit with significant social impact potential. You'll work closely with our leadership team and external development partners to drive our product roadmap and ensure we're building solutions that truly serve our community.

This role is perfect for a seasoned product professional who thrives in a startup-like environment, values mission-driven work, and wants to take ownership over every aspect of product development.

‍

Key Responsibilities
Product Strategy & Roadmap:

Steer end-to-end product and feature development, from ideation to launch
Develop and maintain comprehensive product roadmaps and release schedules
Make recommendations on feature prioritization and release timing
Build, manage, and document product development lifecycle processes and best practices
Continuously optimize existing features
Cross-Functional Leadership:

Serve as the primary liaison with our external development team
Monitor product performance, user feedback, and key metrics
Manage engineering budget
Communicate with key stakeholders and draft key product requirements
Balance user needs, business objectives, and technical constraints
Coordinate with leadership on strategic product initiatives and goals
Quality Assurance & Launch Management:

Design and implement QA processes to ensure clean production deployments
Conduct product testing, UI/UX review, and approval workflows
Establish quality gates and testing protocols for new features and releases
Take ownership of the "ship/no-ship" decision-making process
